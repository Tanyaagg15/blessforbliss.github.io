What is Adoption?

Children adoption is very popular around the world but nowdays parents adoption is also gaining equal importance.

The permanent legal transfer of all parental rights from one person to another person or couple is called Adoption. There is no difference between the rights of adoptive parents or children and the biological parents or children and an adopted childrenor parents have all of the social, legal, emotional and kinship benefits of biological children or parents.
