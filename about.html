What is Adoption?

Children adoption is very popular around the world but nowdays parents adoption is also gaining equal importance.

The permanent legal transfer of all parental rights from one person to another person or couple is called Adoption. There is no difference between the rights of adoptive parentsor children and the biological parents or children and an adopted childrenor parents have all of the social, legal, emotional and kinship benefits of biological children or parents.

  • Adoption provides help to the hopeful parents or children who are not able to have children or parents otherwise.
  • It is popoular option for same-sex people, individual parents or children who do not have children or parents.
  • It provides love,care, happiness to those parents and children who are abandoned by their children and parents.

The best adoptions are those where prospective adoptive parents or children are able to move beyond their personal desires and have the interests of the adopted child or parent in mind.
